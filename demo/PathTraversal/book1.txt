[HTTP Textbook]
A complete and approachable guide to how the web works and its protocols.

The web we all use daily from PCs and smartphones is supported by protocols.
They are what make the often-mentioned "conversation between servers" possible.
So what does it actually mean to "enable conversation"?
This book explains protocols essential not only to the web but to the internet itself,
covering the latest trends in language simple enough for students yet practical for engineers.
